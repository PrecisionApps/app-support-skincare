Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkinCare?

SkinCare is an AI-powered app that provides cosmetic and aesthetic suggestions based on photos you upload. The app analyzes visual patterns like brightness, contrast, and general aesthetic attributes to offer style-based recommendations.

‚ö†Ô∏è Cosmetic Use Only: SkinCare provides cosmetic suggestions for entertainment purposes. It does NOT detect illnesses, analyze skin conditions, or provide medical advice of any kind.
How does the AI analysis work?

When you upload a photo, your 2D image is sent to our secure backend to be processed by a large language model (LLM). The AI analyzes visual patterns like brightness, contrast, and aesthetic attributes to provide cosmetic and style-based suggestions.

‚ö†Ô∏è Cosmetic Only: The AI provides artistic and cosmetic suggestions only. It does NOT detect illnesses, analyze skin conditions, extract biometric data, or perform facial recognition.
What happens to my photos?

Your 2D image file is sent to our secure backend to be processed by a large language model (LLM) for cosmetic suggestions. Images are not permanently stored in databases and are not used to train AI models.

üîí No Biometric Data: We do NOT collect or store biometric identifiers such as facial geometry, facial recognition templates, depth maps, or any data that can uniquely identify you. Only the 2D image you upload is processed.
Does SkinCare detect skin conditions or provide medical advice?

NO. SkinCare provides cosmetic and aesthetic suggestions ONLY. This app does NOT:

  • Analyze or diagnose skin conditions
  • Extract biometric features
  • Perform facial recognition
