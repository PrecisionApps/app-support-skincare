## Description

Quitto is the all-in-one habit-breaking app designed to help you quit smoking, alcohol, porn, social media, gambling, cannabis, vaping, gaming, caffeine, sugar, and more. Built on proven behavioral science and real recovery timelines, Quitto gives you the tools, insights, and daily support you need to break free for good.

Whether you are on day one or day one hundred, Quitto adapts to your journey with a personalized dashboard, AI-powered coaching, craving management tools, and milestone tracking that keeps you motivated every step of the way.

SPECIALIZED RECOVERY MODES

Each habit has its own dedicated experience. Quitting smoking is different from quitting social media, and Quitto knows that. Select your habit and get a fully tailored dashboard with recovery timelines, health metrics, and coping strategies specific to what you are quitting.

Supported habits include Smoking, Alcohol, Porn, Social Media, Gambling, Sugar, Cannabis, Caffeine, Vaping, Gaming, and a customizable Other category.

AI RECOVERY COACH

Talk to your personal AI coach anytime you need support. Whether you are battling a 2am craving, celebrating a milestone, or processing a setback, your coach responds with empathy, evidence-based strategies, and personalized insight based on your progress. Powered by the latest AI technology for fast, thoughtful responses that stream in real time.

REAL-TIME RECOVERY DASHBOARD

Watch your body and mind heal in real time. Your dashboard tracks days clean, money saved, units avoided, time reclaimed, and health metrics specific to your habit. See exactly how far you have come and what is happening inside your body at each stage of recovery.

CRAVING TRACKER AND ANALYSIS

Log cravings when they hit. Track intensity, triggers, and outcomes. Over time, Quitto identifies your patterns, showing you your peak craving hours, most common triggers, and your success rate at resisting urges. Understanding your cravings is the first step to beating them.

RECOVERY JOURNAL

Process your journey through guided journaling. Track your mood daily, reflect on challenges and victories, and build a personal record of your transformation. Journaling is one of the most effective tools in recovery, and Quitto makes it simple.

MILESTONE SYSTEM

Celebrate your progress with meaningful milestones. From your first 24 hours to your first full year, each milestone marks a real achievement in your recovery. Unlock them as you go and see how close you are to the next one.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS

Keep your motivation visible with beautiful home screen widgets. See your days clean, streak, money saved, and progress toward your next milestone at a glance, right on your home screen. Available in small, medium, and large sizes.

SMART NOTIFICATIONS

Receive morning motivation to start your day strong and evening reflection prompts to celebrate your progress. Get milestone reminders as you approach new achievements. All notifications are fully customizable.

HEALTH INTEGRATION

Quitto connects with Apple Health to display real heart rate and blood oxygen data on your recovery dashboard, giving you tangible proof of how your body is healing.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your recovery data stays on your device. Quitto does not sell or share your personal information. Your journey is yours alone.
