Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

⚠️ Important: Quitto does NOT provide medical advice. The app is for educational and general wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Introduction

Quitto ("the App") is an addiction recovery and habit-tracking application developed by Kaan YILDIZ (Precision Apps). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information You Provide

  • Profile Data: Your first name and quit date, stored locally on your device
  • Habit Data: Habit type, daily amount, cost per unit, and motivation text
  • Journal Entries: Personal journal entries you write within the App
  • Craving Logs: Craving intensity, triggers, and coping strategies you record
  • AI Coach Messages: Messages you send to the AI Coach feature

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect biometric data
  • We do NOT collect precise location data
  • We do NOT collect contacts, photos, or browsing history
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share personal data for advertising
  • We do NOT use your data for AI model training

Data Storage

Local Storage

All personal data (profile, habits, journal entries, craving logs, milestones, and coach messages) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

AI Coach Data

When you use the AI Coach, your messages are sent to a secure third-party AI service (OpenAI) to generate responses. These messages are:

  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Not stored permanently by the AI service beyond the session
  • Not used to train AI models
  • Processed solely to provide you with a coaching response

Note: The AI Coach is not a medical professional. Its responses are for general wellness support only. All health-related information includes citations from recognized organizations.

Subscription Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment information.
