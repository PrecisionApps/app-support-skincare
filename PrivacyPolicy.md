## Introduction

Quitto ("the App") is an addiction recovery and habit-tracking application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

**Quitto does NOT provide medical advice.** The App is for educational and general wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Profile Data:** Your first name and quit date, stored locally on your device.
- **Habit Data:** Habit type, daily amount, cost per unit, and motivation text.
- **Journal Entries:** Personal journal entries you write within the App.
- **Craving Logs:** Craving intensity, triggers, and coping strategies you record.
- **AI Coach Conversations:** Messages you send to the AI Coach feature.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Anonymous Analytics:** We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., feature usage frequency) to improve the App. This data cannot identify you personally.
- **Crash Reports:** Anonymous crash data may be collected through Apple's standard crash reporting to help us fix bugs.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** collect biometric data.
- We do **NOT** collect precise location data.
- We do **NOT** collect your contacts, photos, or browsing history.
- We do **NOT** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
- We do **NOT** use your data for AI model training.

---

## How Your Data Is Stored

### Local Storage

All personal data (profile, habits, journal entries, craving logs, milestones, and coach messages) is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

### AI Coach Data

When you use the AI Coach feature, your messages are sent to a secure third-party AI service (OpenAI) to generate responses. These messages are:

- Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
- Not stored permanently by the AI service beyond the session.
- Not used to train AI models.
- Processed solely to provide you with a coaching response.

The AI Coach is **not a medical professional** and its responses are for general wellness support only. All health-related information includes citations to recognized medical organizations (e.g., AHA, CDC, WHO, NIDA, APA) for your reference.

### Subscription Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data at any time within the App.
- **Delete** all your data by using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings.
- **Export** your data is not currently supported but may be added in future updates.
- **Opt out** of notifications via iOS Settings or within the App.

---

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- **AI Coach Messages:** Transmitted to generate responses and not permanently stored by the AI service.
- **Subscription Data:** Managed by Apple and RevenueCat per their respective privacy policies.
